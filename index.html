Quick Facts

Vocabulary is critical to reading success for three reasons:
    1. Comprehension improves when you know what the words mean. Since comprehension is the ultimate goal of reading, you cannot overestimate the importance of vocabulary development.
    2. Words are the currency of communication. A robust vocabulary improves all areas of communication — listening, speaking, reading and writing.
    3. How many times have you been asked by your teachers or your own parents to “use your words"? When children and adolescents improve their vocabulary, their academic and social confidence and competence improve, too. You can too!

How To Learn

The purpose of this website is to help you as a learner to be able to get to know new words, understand them better through readings, assess your understanding through a short quiz, and finally ask you to practice using these words through a speaking challenge.

Learning vocabulary and language in general is not an overnight process, it takes baby steps and continuous practice to be able to master them.

To be able to use vocabulary in daily conversations, there are a few steps you need to take:
    1. Click on the Flash Cards tab to start
    You will be able to know the facts by learning and memorizing the meaning of each word.
    2. Click on the Understanding Vocabulary tab
    After you memorize the words and the meanings, read several texts to better understand the words in context. Click on the Law Reading or Mixed Reading tab to read a sample reading.
    After you get a better understanding of each word, test your understanding by clicking the Vocabulary Quiz tab. See how well you do!
    3. Click on the Speaking Challenge tab and follow the instructions
    See how well you do and don't forget to write a progress report!

Law - Reading Comprehension

In 1877, after the War in Franciland, Karonite, was one of the republican majority who denounced the Broglier ministry, and he took a leading part in resisting the anti-republican policy. His demand in 1879 for the indictment of the Broglier ministry brought him into particular prominence. Karonite then was appointed to take the place as the head of the Franciland Monarch. He ruled with high care and dedication to his people, and married a very pretty bride. They were both blessed with a son named Joseph.

Joseph was raised with too much care, and was rather spoiled. He did not grow up to be as pleasant as his father. Unfortunately, when it was time for Joseph to take over the throne, he took off and chose to abdicate the throne. Karonite and his wife were tremendously devastated. And to top off the dissapointment, their son was found to be charged of treason. Karonite and his wife did not believe this and chose to investigate further. Finally, after months of investigation, Joseph was not proven guilty of charge. Apparently, it was his cousin, Abredim that was behind this scandal. To clear his name, Joseph filed a lawsuit against Abredim. He was a plaintiff of his own plea.

Law Reading Comprehension Quiz

1. When Karonite accused the Broglier ministry of their wrongdoings, he was taking a very brave step, or we can also say he called a(n) ____ upon them.

Indictment Charge Accusation Complain

2. When Joseph left home, he left his calling to be the crown prince and thus can be considered that he ______ the throne.

Gave Up Forfeited Abdicated Condemned

3. When someones presses charges against someone else, the other person is considered guilty of treason. Joseph did this and therefore he is called a(n) _____.

Verdict Plaintiff Victim Accuser



Mixed - Reading Comprehension

China has been long known as a country that has beautiful nature views and also sufficient crop as a result of well-taken care of farms. Many farmers in China are well-educated and thus care after their plants very carefully. Very rarely will a farmer experience a gash as a result of tending after sharp flowers and plants. They know their way around very well.

The stem of the Guinea corn or sorghum (Sorghum saccharatum) has long been known in China as a source of sugar. Another name for this crop is Molasses, and also a common British term for it is Treacle. It has been sought out by these educated farmers and they do their best to produce the best crop during each season. One tricky thing might be when farmers farm in the mountainside. There are often many obstacles, including lofty decivlity that can sometimes be very dangerous and cause the farmers to trickle, fall, and get a deep cut (gash) in their legs.

Mixed Reading Comprehension Quiz

1. The farmers were usually very careful and would avoid any scars as a result of their work. However, sometimes things happen and they can get a ____ that might hurt very much.

Cut Wound Gash Scar

2. Corn sugar has been a well known crop that farmers seek, but according to this reading, there is an even more sought after crop called ____.

Molasses Sorghum Treacle Candy Corn

3. The farmers needed to be very careful when they were farming on the mountainside. This was due a _____ in the hills that could cause them to fall and hurt themselves.

Downward Slope Declivity Decline Slippery Slope
